About Nate

Nate's GitHub and LinkedIn

Well you wanted to know so here goes.

Hi, I’m Nate Hall. As an identical twin, growing up there were always two sets of hands ready and willing whenever we wanted to accomplish something mischievous. After graduating from high school and my twin joined the Marines, I was left with only one set of hands when working towards goals. Not that college allows collaboration on essays or exams anyway. I began devouring information in any way I could find it, and subsequently sought ways to make this knowledge help me work smarter not harder.

Several years, many classes, a Pedagogy degree, a wonderful wife, and adorable one year old later, I’ve found passion in Data Science. I love learning and wrangling and applying programming languages to make systems work more efficiently and ferret out the most useful and poignant data. The simpler the code and the better the output, the happier I am.

My goal here on this blog is to share what I’ve learned, discovered, and created along the way and maybe it will help someone reach that aha moment to create smart code and make new discoveries.
